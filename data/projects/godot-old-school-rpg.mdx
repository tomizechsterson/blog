This is a top-down, grid-based RPG that I intend to be slightly larger than what would come from a tutorial. It uses an
awesome asset pack found [here](https://kenney.nl/data/itch/preview/), and is well worth the money for anyone needing assets
for a project. The purpose here is to learn some more features of the Godot engine and push the scope out a bit further.
Maybe someday I'll be able to manage something that can justify a price tag above $0.00, but for now, I expect this one will
also be available for free on GameJolt.

> [!NOTE]
> As soon as there's _anything_ worth showing, I'll get screenshots in here and make an image for the project card. We
> really are that early on in the project right now, such that we literally only have grid-based movement and tile
> collision implemented. And that's it.
